ABOUT SHE

'SHE' an abbreviation for SCHEME FOR HER EMPOWERMENT.Generally it is observed that when a coders are asked to team up girls often lack the confidence to come forward.Our aim is to give tools and community for women to understand technology and build their ideas.Learn sketching,prototyping,basic programming and enter to the world of technology.In short we try develop a learning platform for the community of femle coders of GCEK..

WHY SHE?

She consists of mentorship-based volunteer groups with the aim of inspiring women in technology careers.A common platform with many peers having same ideologies help us to make the journey through the tech world much more interesting and lively.The coordinators of SHE have well identified the value of our resourceful alumini and have taken guidelines from them.A perfect linking between theory and practicals is essential.Building up such a perfect base is quite difficult.If yoy don't have started yet come join us.Let us move together correcting each other,clapping for each other and thus climb up the hill.
